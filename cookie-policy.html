Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 December 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how GymStoreOnline.com ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide analytics, and deliver personalised experiences. Cookies cannot run programmes or deliver viruses to your device.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, session management, and remembering your cookie consent preference. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the website cannot function without them.

  • gsoCookieConsent — Remembers your cookie consent decision (stored in localStorage)

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. These cookies help us understand which pages are most popular, how visitors find us, and how we can improve the site. The information collected is aggregated and anonymous.

  • _ga — Google Analytics. Distinguishes unique users. Expires: 2 years.
  • _gid — Google Analytics. Distinguishes users. Expires: 24 hours.
  • _gat — Google Analytics. Throttles request rate. Expires: 1 minute.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on or by declining analytics cookies when prompted.

2.3 Third-Party Cookies (Amazon)

When you click on an Amazon affiliate link on our site, you are directed to Amazon.com. Amazon may set their own cookies on your device. These cookies are governed by Amazon's Privacy Notice and Cookie Policy. We do not have control over these cookies.

Amazon uses cookies to track affiliate referrals (to ensure we receive commission credit for qualifying purchases), personalise your Amazon experience, and for fraud prevention.

2.4 YouTube Embedded Videos

Our website includes embedded YouTube videos. YouTube (owned by Google) may set cookies when you interact with these videos. These cookies are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced embed mode where possible to minimise cookie setting before you interact with the video.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

3.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, a cookie banner will appear allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

3.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find cookie controls in your browser's "Settings", "Preferences", or "Privacy" menu. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

3.3 Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out
  • YourOnlineChoices (EU)
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out (US)

4. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Our website does not currently respond to DNT signals, but you can manage your cookie preferences as described above.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or law. The "last updated" date at the top will indicate when the policy was last revised.
